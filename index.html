Personal Statement

    I am a graduate student at Korea University College of Medicine, Department of Biomedical Sciences. My research interests lie in unraveling the mechanisms of cancer and other diseases through the analysis of omics data, including Whole Exome Sequencing (WES), bulk RNA sequencing, and single-cell RNA sequencing.
    Utilizing various methodologies and leveraging machine learning techniques, my long-term goal is to become an expert in bioinformatics with a focus on disease-specific and cancer-specific causal gene discovery. Through the graduate program, I aim to conduct cutting-edge research to achieve this goal.
